28 - 31 May 2021
CANCELED

Information about the Book Fair in Paris in French here.
Informations sur le Salon du livre à Paris en français ici. 

Unfortunately, the Paris Book Fair was canceled this year due to the COVID-19 pandemic. We are currently negotiating with the Czech Center in Paris on the possible implementation of some events within the accompanying program.

The Paris Book Fair was established in 1981 at the initiative of the French Publishers' Association (Syndicat national de l’édition) and over the years has become the largest cultural event in book culture and industry in France.

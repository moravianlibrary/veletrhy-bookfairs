Warsaw
Book Fair

9-12 September 2021

Information about the Book Fair in Warsaw in Polish here.
Informacje o Warszawskie Targi Książki w języku polskim tutaj.

The Warsaw Book Fair (Warszawskie Targi Książki) is the largest Polish book festival, this year's event will take place from 9 to 12. September 2021 in the outdoor area in front of the Palace of Culture and Science in the center of Warsaw. The Czech Republic is the main guest this year. In 2020, the fair was cancelled due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
Every year, around a thousand writers, translators, illustrators, journalists and representatives of the book industry go to the fair. The Czech Republic will bring to the eleventh year of book fair 12 authors selected by the Dramaturgical Council. As part of the accompanying program, will be presented to Polish readers the other 3 authors. 
The hosting of the Czech Republic at the Warsaw Book Fair is being prepared by the Moravian Library on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the Czech Republic, which initiated and co-finances the project. The project will also be supported by the Czech Center Warsaw, the Embassy of the Czech Republic in Warsaw and the Czech Literary Center.

ACADEMIA

The publishing house was established in the year 1953, formerly as the publishing house of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences. The name Academia has been used since 1966. Today Academia has a leading position among publishers in the Czech Republic. Academia publishes original scientific monographs and works by Czech scientists, also foreign classics in science, popular science, non-fiction, encyclopaedias, as well as quality fiction in the original Czech and in translation.

HOST — vydavatelství, s. r. o.

Host is one of the largest independent publishing houses in the Czech Republic, established nearly 30 years ago. Host currently publishes about 150 titles annually, in genres including general fiction, SF and fantasy, crime fiction, thrillers, children’s literature, popular science, specialist literature, poetry. Host Czech authors are among the top performers in the Czech Republic – in terms of copies sold, awards won, reception by critics and readers alike, and a number of translations.

INFOA

INFOA is a wholly independent publishing house based in the Czech Republic, which is dedicated to producing quality language and children materials. Founded in 1992, the company has enjoyed steady, rapid growth and currently has a list of over 2000 titles. INFOA language titles cater for both adult learners and children, at a wide range of levels and languages. In addition INFOA's list includes children's board books, activity books, guides and colouring books for children as well us adults.

Spolek pro vydávání revue Prostor

Revue Prostor is a literary-cultural magazine with a samizdat past focusing on pressing cultural and social issues of today's world. The concept of each half-yearly issue is unique both in terms of content and graphics, but also in terms of the circle of contributors, thus ensuring a rich conceptual, aesthetic and opinion diversity. The texts presented in the revue have mainly the form of essays, short stories, poems, dramas or excerpts from prose and dramatic texts. Thanks to its thematic diversity, the magazine Prostor succeeds in reaching a wide range of audiences.

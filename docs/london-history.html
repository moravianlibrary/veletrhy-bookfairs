2019 (March 12-14)  

 On Tuesday, March 12, 2019, the London Book Fair will open its gates to visitors, which will last until Thursday, March 14. The Czech Republic is represented here by the stand of the Ministry of Culture, which is traditionally organized by the Moravian Library.

The London Book Fair is known as a universal fair, in which, in addition to presenting the authors and their book novelties, emphasis is placed on accompanying seminars and conferences, which focus not only on authors but also on other aspects of the book market.

2017 (March 13-16)

The spring season of international book fairs begins on March 13. The London Book Fair, which will last until Thursday 16 March, opens its doors to visitors. The Czech Republic is represented here by the stand of the Ministry of Culture, which is traditionally organized by the Moravian Regional Library.

The London Book Fair is known as a universal fair, in which, in addition to presenting the authors and their novelties, the emphasis is on accompanying seminars and conferences. This year, those interested are offered up to eight conferences, which are focused on the sale of rights, through the issue of publishing to children's literature. In the spotlight, next to the main country, Poland this year, are four award-winning writers, Michael Morpurgo, Roddy Doyle, Olga Tokarczuk and Andrzej Sapkowski.

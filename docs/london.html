The London
 Book Fair

April 5 to 7, 2022

The London Book Fair this year will open on 5 April 2022.

​ The London Book Fair is known as an universal book fair, where the emphasis is placed on seminars and conferences, as well as the presentation of authors and their new works. This year, visitors can participate at a number of conferences and seminars focusing on authors and other aspects of publishing business.

at the book fair

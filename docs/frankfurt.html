Frankfurt
Book Fair


16-20 October 2024 HALL 4.1 D76

The seventy-sixth edition of the Frankfurt Book Fair (Frankfurter Buchmesse), the largest book fair all over the world, will physically take place this year from 16 to 20 October 2024. The Czech Republic will not be absent from the fair.

The Czech national stand includes a presentation of Czech literature and Czech book culture, prepared on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the Czech Republic by the Moravian Library in Brno. It acquaints you with the current form and themes of Czech literature through a selection from current book production that has received awards in literary competitions (for example, Magnesia litera, Zlatá stuha). You can see publications from the Most Beautiful Czech Books collection, which received awards in a competition organized by the Ministry of Culture of the Czech Republic and the Museum of Czech Literature. The exhibition features new books of contemporary Czech prose, poetry, comics and literature for children and youth, as well as new translations supported by the Ministry of Culture of the Czech Republic and books from Milan Kundera’s Library.
At the same time, the national stand offers the current production of some important Czech publishers. Exhibitors will present specialist and popular science books, scientific monographs, encyclopedias, language publications, textbooks, books on art and architecture, children's literature, fiction, science fiction, fantasy books and poetry.

EUROMEDIA GROUP, a. s.

Euromedia Group (founded in 1991) is one of the largest companies operating in the Czech book market. It publishes over 700 new titles per year in a wide range of genres in various imprints, including an imprint for audiobooks. EMG also owns the largest book distribution network in the Czech Republic, supplying the booksellers, bookselling networks and e-shops with the production of the majority of Czech publishers. In addition, it has a chain of bookstores (Luxor) and a subscribers book club (Knižní klub).

GRADA PUBLISHING, a. s.

Grada Publishing is the most prominent Czech publishing house in non-fiction. Since 2015 GRADA has also been focusing on fiction with the new imprint COSMOPOLIS to publish important foreign titles and the best contemporary Czech literature. In 2015 GRADA introduced BAMBOOK for children’s literature and ALFERIA for alternative schools of philosophy, lifestyle and self-help. In 2018, Grada acquired METAFORA which brings together fiction, biographies, hobby and body-mind-spirit. Now, GRADA is one of the three biggest publishers in the country with more than 400 new titles/year.

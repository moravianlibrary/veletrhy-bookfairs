2020

The seventy-second year of the Frankfurt Book Fair (Frankfurter Buchmesse), which is the most important European book event of the autumn, took place from 14th to 18th October 2020. With regard to this year's situation around the COVID-19 pandemic, the fair has been digitized. All national presentations, including co-exhibitors, did not take place physically, but were transferred to the online space.

The Frankfurt Book Fair complemented the BOOKFEST City festival, which filled the streets of Frankfurt with chamber literary and cultural events for five days, and many events also took place online. Among the 80 events, Alena Mornštajnová presented herself to the German audience for the first time on October 16 with her novel Hana translated into German (published by Wieser, 2020, translated by Raija Hauck), who joined Martin Krafl, program coordinator Moravian Library in Brno virtually. On the German side, moderator Mirko Schwanitz discussed with them.

The Czech national presentation is provided by the Moravian Regional Library in Brno on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the Czech Republic.
